PBC DEVICE

ECM Device stands for PETROL BIKE CONTROL DEVICE.This is the device for 2 wheeler petrol bike.Basically this device is based on INTERNATE OF THINGS(IOT). Using this device you can control your bike through server as well as bluetooth.You can control this device through laptop,pc,ipad,mobile depends on your interest. Also you can control through your mobile bluetooth using bluetooth function this device has bluetooth functionality.


SPECIFICATIONS OF PETROL BIKE CONTROL DEVICE


  1. UNLOCK:-

    You can unlock your bike through server by sending unlock command as well as you can send command for unlock using bluetooth AAP using your smart phone,and you can moniter it on your mobile screen.


  2. START:-

    After sending unlock command only you can start your bike using bike key.


  3. STOP:-

    After reaching to your desired destination you can turn off your bike. Once you turn off your bike you will not be able to again turn on using bike key.you will have to again send unlock through server or bluetooth.


  4. IMMOBILIZE:-

    You can immobilize your vechicle by sending immobilize command, once you immobilized you vechicle, your vechicle acceletor will stop working after that your vechicle will not move from that place. After sending immobilized command you will get a notificaion as a command that you have immobilzed your vechicle.


  5. HORN:-

    You can honk your vechicle by sending horn command through server as well as bluetooth.


  6. GPS:-

    This device has also GPS.You can easily track your vechicle by sending locator command.

ADVANTAGES OF PBC DEVICE

  1. This device has very low power consumption, During sleep mode it needs only 0.23 AMP current and during working(whenever you will send command) only that time it needs 0.81 AMP current and voltage rating is 12 volts.

  2. You can fix this device in your petrol bike, you do not need any extra battery for this, directly you can connect with 2-wheeler battery.

  3. Maintenance cost is very low.

  4. Bying cost is very low.

  5. Security of your vechicle, because we are providing GPS.

  6. If your bike is in non-networking zone than you can controls it through bluetooth AAP.
